Scope of the Problem:

1 in 5 children live in poverty.

People in poverty are more likely to have negative health outcomes,
lower levels of education, and are more likely to be incarcerated.

People who are in poverty, often have a shorter lifespan then those who do not.

13.5% of Americans (43.1million) live in poverty.
-CDC 2015

Poverty often impacts education, graduation rates, and future economic success.
